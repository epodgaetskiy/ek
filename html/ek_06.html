Was born in 1965 in Rostov-on-Don.

In 1986 graduated the Rostov specialized school of arts.

In 1991 graduated the Rostov State Conservatoire, opera singer specialty (mezzo-soprano).

In 2003 completed the course of the Central Saint Martins College of Art & Design in London.

In 2004 graduated the Moscow State University, “Sociology Of Fashion” specialty.

Since 1991 lives and works in Moscow, Russia and Palma, Spain.

2003 – Creative director of the Russian Golf Association. Launches the deluxe class golf clothing line “GOLFOREST”.

2005 – Becomes the co-founder of the first ever golf-related Awards in Russia.

2006 – Launches the concept clothing line “Elena Kozhevnikova”. Takes part in the international Volvo Moscow Fashion Week.

2008 – Creates the first concept jewellery collection. Designs the prize for the British young designers contest Fashion Fringe.

2009-2011 – Designs several interiors for the deluxe class apartments, using her own painting and collages as a part of the furnishing.

2012 – motivated by her friends and partners, launches the website elena-kozhevnikova.com containing the works executed in mixed media, oil, pastel.

2013 - 2014 - represented by Agora Gallery, New York and Artist's Haven Gallery, Florida.

2014 - 2015 - represented by Agora Gallery, New York.

2015 - present days - represented by Agora Gallery, New York and PAKS Gallery, Vienna, Austria.
